 Your Pawn’s Defaults

These are the specifics of what your pawn will have unless you specifically ask me to change something or purchase something.



Apparel

Your pawn will spawn wearing something - who knows. They will wear what they spawn in until either a default outfit is available or you arrange something else to wear.  Once the colony is up and running, the following will be the default outfit available for every pawn to change into.


• Quality: Normal to Masterwork

• Hit Points: 51% to 100%

• Materials: Common

• Skin: Jumpsuit

• Middle: Boots

• Outer: Parka


If you spawn an article of clothing or armor, the materials and the quality is random. If you would like something specific, please purchase the material you’d like to be used and include instructions.



Weapons

The Simple Sidearms mod is active. All pawns will be set to “use weapons according to skill or passion” as far as Melee or Shooting being the primary weapon unless otherwise instructed. 


Sometimes pawns spawn with weapons, sometimes not. If your pawn does not spawn with any weapons or the weapons spawned are of awful or poor quality, the default weapons will be the following:

• Quality: Normal to Masterwork

• Hit Points: 51% to 100%

• Melee: Steel Knife

• Shooting: Long Bow



Duties

Your pawn will be assigned to do whatever it is you are best at. If there are no clear skills, you will be assigned to either haul, clean, smelt, cut stone, or write as your skills dictate.



Schedule

Unless your pawn is a Night Owl or you specifically as for the night shift, this will be your schedule.



Sleeping Accommodations

Your bed may or may not be in a barracks. Private bedrooms will be assigned on a “first spawned, first served” basis as they are built.  If you already have a private room and would like to add special things to it or upgrade the furniture, you must purchase them or the materials to make them.


Quality: Normal to Legendary

Materials: Stone Blocks, Wood, or Steel

Bed: Simple Bed or Double Bed

End Table: Yes

Dresser: Yes



Food

The default food restriction is for meals and pemmican only - no raw food, kibble, or corpses. If you’d like to change it, these are the options:


• Raw: raw meats, veggies, animal products, and kibble. No pemmican or corpses. Perfect for an aesthetic

• Cannibal Meals: Includes all of the default options as well as cannibal versions of each.

• Strict Cannibal: Only includes options that include human meat. If this is your food restriction and there is no human meat or human meat products available, you will starve.

• Special Policies: Is your pawn on a “bananas only” diet? Cool. If you’d like your pawn to have any kind of a special diet, a policy can be created if you purchase the “Special RimWorld Pawn Policy” under Channel Points.



Drugs

The default drug policy is no drugs whatsoever. Why? Because we make drugs to sell them, not use them. Plus most tend to slow down or otherwise hinder a pawn. 


• Social Drugs: Those with this policy are allowed to use wine, beer, mead, and smokeleaf during recreation times. If you would like to be on the “social” policy, you need to let me know.

• Chemical Interest/Fascination: If your pawn is spawned with one of these traits, the colony will provide one smokeleaf joint every two days to keep you from losing it. If you choose to add this trait to your pawn, you must request a special policy and supply the drugs. 

• Luciferium: Your pawn will only receive Luci if you specifically ask for it and purchase it. A communal supply will not be kept on hand. If the colony runs out of Luci and you miss a dose - that is on you.

• Special Policies: You want to be a stoner or addicted to Go-juice? Cool. A policy can be created if you purchase the “Special RimWorld Pawn Policy” under Channel Points.


Addictions

You are responsible for providing any drugs your pawn becomes addicted to. You will be placed into a cryocasket when your withdrawal symptoms start to severely interfere with your colonist’s mood until you return/send more.
