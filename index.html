Welcome! We must be playing RimWorld. Want to get in on the fun? Twitch Toolkit is a mod that allows viewers to interact directly with RimWorld. 


Getting Coins

You earn coins by simply watching the stream. These coins can then be used to purchase and send things directly to the game.

Want more coins? Use your channel points to purchase them!

Want even MOAR coins? Subscribers earn an extra 25 coins per interval and a 1.5 coin bonus multiplier. Plus subs get an extra vote in any in game poll.


Commands

There are LOTS of commands available to you. They range from checking your coin balance to scheduling your own pawn for surgery to install a bionic leg. You can see the full list here: https://alsoknownast.github.io/T-TTK/commands


Here are some of the most common:

!bal - check balance and karma rate

!buy - purchase an item or event for the colony

!backpack - purchase an item for your pawn

!modsettings - how many coins are being earned and global settings



Mod List

I like mods. Lots and lots of mods. They are mostly small mods that change tiny things for QOL or visual or organizing. I try to keep this as updated as possible. See the full list here: https://alsoknownast.github.io/T-TTK/modlist

Animal policy:

If you purchase an animal, it will be sold or slaughtered. 

My computer is not healthy enough to maintain large quantities of stuff moving around on the screen. If you have a pawn in the colony, you may have no more than one “pet” of any animal type, however, you are expected to feed them.


There are two ways to participate in the stream using purchased items. 


Colony Support Specialist

If you’d like to participate, but don’t want to commit to having a pawn, you can simply buy stuff the colony needs. Go to the store and check out the categories for Items, Events, and Animals. Not sure what to buy? Well, we almost always could basics like steel, components, and meals. If you want to splurge on luxury items, we would never turn down some extra attomachinery, picomachinery, or nanomachinery. Beyond that? Just ask! We might have a pressing need at the moment.


If you are purchasing something for the colony, use

!buy [item] [quantity] [optional: note for T]

Example: !buy pokertable 1 I want to see a pawn play poker by itself. Because that is a thing. Apparently.



Getting a Pawn

You can put yourself right in the action!

Use: !buy pawn

Cost: 3000


When you purchase a pawn, it will spawn a new colony member with random traits, looks, clothes - random everything. It will also have your Twitch name as its nickname.If you purchase yourself a pawn, I expect you’ll take care of it. I will provide some basics (like food, clothing, and shelter), but if you want anything beyond the sheer basics you will need to provide it. To see exactly what I’ll provide, check out https://alsoknownast.github.io/T-TTK/pawndefaults


If you are purchasing something for your pawn, use

!backpack [item] [quantity] [optional: note for T]

Example: !backpack picomachinery 240 Please craft me some heavy plate armor made from this for my pawn.


You can check many of your pawn’s details using commands. Check the command for the full list, but here are some common ones:

!mypawnstats - Shows your current stats for the different skills

!mypawnhealth - Shows an overview of your pawn’s health

!mypawngear - Shows an overview of your pawn’s gear


Body Part Upgrades

If you are purchasing a new body part, organ, or anything else that needs to be surgically implanted, you can buy the part and schedule yourself for surgery in one command.

!surgery [item to buy]

Example: !surgery syntheticliver
